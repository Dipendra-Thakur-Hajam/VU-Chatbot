SOURCE: Vishwakarma University Pune
CATEGORY: Admissions

Required documents for admission:

- 10th Mark Sheet
- 12th Mark Sheet / Graduation Mark Sheet
- Transfer Certificate
- Migration Certificate (if applicable)
- Government-issued Photo ID (Aadhar/Passport)
- Passport-size photographs
- Entrance exam scorecard (if applicable)
- Caste / Income Certificate (for scholarship candidates)

All documents must be uploaded online and originals presented during admission confirmation.
