Eligibility criteria vary by program:

Undergraduate Programs:
- Candidate must have passed 10+2 from a recognized board
- Minimum aggregate: 50% (45% for reserved categories)
- Relevant subject background required for technical programs

Postgraduate Programs:
- Bachelor’s degree in a relevant discipline
- Minimum aggregate: 50%
- Entrance exam or interview may be required

Doctoral Programs (PhD):
- Master’s degree in relevant field
- Research proposal submission
- Entrance test and interview mandatory
