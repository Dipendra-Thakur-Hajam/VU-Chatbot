The admission process at Vishwakarma University (VU Pune) is conducted online through the official admission portal.

Step 1: Online Registration  
Applicants must register on the VU admission portal using a valid email ID and mobile number.

Step 2: Application Form Submission  
Candidates fill in personal, academic, and program-related details and upload required documents such as mark sheets and ID proof.

Step 3: Entrance Examination  
Applicants may be required to appear for the Vishwakarma University National Entrance Test (VUNET) or submit scores of national-level entrance exams, depending on the program.

Step 4: Document Verification  
Uploaded documents are verified by the admission team. Incomplete or incorrect documents may lead to rejection.

Step 5: Counseling / Interview  
Shortlisted candidates are invited for counseling or personal interviews (online or offline).

Step 6: Offer Letter  
Selected candidates receive a provisional admission offer letter.

Step 7: Fee Payment & Confirmation  
Admission is confirmed after payment of the first installment of fees and verification of original documents.
